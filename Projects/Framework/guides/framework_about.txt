About Framework

Framework is a man page system for The Engine ecosystem.
Framework has a TUI interface, a seach bar,
and articles about every question you could have about the ecosystem.

To search you can press "/" or "f".
When searching you can press "enter" to go to the first article in the results.
You can also press "esc" to be able to scroll the search results.

At any time you can press "q" to quit the program.

You scroll with the arrow keys in both the pages search and the actual pages.
